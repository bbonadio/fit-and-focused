Thank you for installing Fit and Focused!

The addon works as follows. Every time you load an unproductive website (facebook etc.), a popup will show you an exercise to make before allowing you on the page.

In the next page, you will be able to choose the websites you want to block from a list, but you can always add more easily while you are browsing by clicking on the addon logo.

You can also choose the length of an exercise session and the time during which the access is granted after completing a workout.
